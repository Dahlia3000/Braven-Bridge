The gap between ready and the real interview

Braven Bridge is an AI-powered preparation layer that gets Fellows interview-ready before the moments Braven already creates — without replacing a single one of them.

Try the live demo → 🤝 Built for Braven Fellows in Chicago
The Problem

Skills without reps don't stick.

Braven's Accelerator equips Fellows with a resume, a LinkedIn, and real career skills. But between finishing the course and sitting in a real interview, most Fellows get just one practice round — a single mock interview event. That's not enough reps to build real confidence.

First-generation students don't have a parent who works in tech to run practice sessions at the kitchen table. They don't have a family friend at the company to give them the inside track. The gap isn't knowledge — it's practice.

🔁

Not enough repetition

One mock interview event builds awareness. Dozens of reps build fluency. Fellows need more practice than any volunteer program can provide at scale.

🎯

Generic preparation

Practice questions should be tailored to the specific companies Fellows are targeting — not one-size-fits-all. Generic prep produces generic answers.

⏱️

Mentors arrive cold

Professional Mentors spend precious 1:1 time learning where a Fellow is — time better spent coaching them forward.

How It Works

Four stages. One clear arc.

Braven Bridge slots into Braven's existing post-course support phase. It doesn't replace anything — it makes every existing touchpoint more powerful.

The Design Philosophy

AI handles scale. Humans handle what matters.

Bridge was designed with a clear principle: AI does the things humans can't do at scale, and humans do the things AI can't do at all.

Claude handles

  • Unlimited practice rounds, available anytime
  • Company-specific question tailoring
  • Immediate structured feedback on every answer
  • PAR framework coaching and story structure
  • Session summaries for mentors
  • Career story refinement prompts

Humans handle

  • Knowing the student behind the profile
  • Coaching presence, energy, and delivery
  • Making warm introductions to employers
  • Emotional support and encouragement
  • Judgment calls on readiness
  • Relationships that open doors

"In a Braven Bridge session, Claude builds your confidence through repetition. In a session with your mentor, you'll work on the things only another person can see — your energy, your presence, and the story only you can tell."

About This Project
Claude Corps Fellowship Applicant

Hi, I'm Dahlia.

I'm a recent graduate and the first in my family and friend group to work in tech. I know firsthand what it feels like to have the skills but not the network — to be ready for a room you've never been invited into before.

I built Braven Bridge as a portfolio concept for the Claude Corps Fellowship, with the goal of being placed at Braven in Chicago. I designed it to solve a real gap in Braven's model without replacing a single thing that makes Braven work — the human relationships, the mentorship, and the community.

This project sits at the intersection of UX design and AI application — two things I believe belong together, especially in service of students who deserve every advantage we can build for them.

📬 Sent to Aimée Eubanks Davis, CEO of Braven

"I've been researching Braven's model and I'm designing an AI-assisted concept around what I see as a real gap: the bridge between what students build in the Accelerator and the moment they actually connect with an employer partner. The network and confidence piece resonates with me personally as the first amongst my friends and family to work in tech. I'd love to be wrong or redirected — is the student-to-employer connection actually the hardest part, or is there a more pressing challenge I should be designing toward?"
